Services Provided

Legal Consulting & Expert Witness Services

Mr. Foster provides security consulting and expert witness services to law firms representing both plaintiffs and defendants in premises liability and negligent security litigation. His work focuses on foreseeability, standard of care, and the defensibility of security programs central to case evaluation, expert disclosure, and trial strategy.

Evaluation & Security Risk Analysis

In-depth security risk assessments identify and prioritize vulnerabilities commonly at issue in negligent security claims, including environmental risk factors, crime patterns, operational practices, and the reasonableness of security measures in place at the time of an incident.

Surveys & Standard-of-Care Assessments

Comprehensive security surveys evaluate existing security measures, policies, procedures, staffing, and physical safeguards against recognized industry standards, accepted practices, and regulatory requirements.

Program Analysis & Defensibility

Evaluation of how security resources were designed, implemented, and managed, including whether measures were reasonably integrated to address foreseeable risks and how decisions can be clearly explained and defended in litigation.
